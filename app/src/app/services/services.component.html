Our Services

Servicing

There are multiple services types. Interim service, full service and major service

After Market Parts

Parts such as exhausts, spoiler, alloys, front grill, and much more can be sold individually or can be fitted to the vehicle at an additional cost.

Body kit

Body kits are sold according to the make and model of the car. Can help to increase the engine performance and the appearance of the car. The kits can be ordered on customer’s demand if in case, the particular model's kit is unavailable.

Car Body Wrap

Vinyl wrap sheets are used to cover the original paint work without any damages to original paint giving the vehicle a superior and a wealthy look

Windows and head/tail lights tint

Window tints and head/ tails lights can help to stop the harsh glare of sun entering the vehicle. The tinting of the lights gives the car a complete over all look to match with the window tint.

Wheels and alloys

Alloys give the car a balanced ride and handling. The alloy wheels are lighter and put less strain on suspension, thus increases the life of suspension. Alloys can act as the boots of the car, which gives the car a sporty look

Service

There are multiple servicing options that we provide to the customers.

  • Interim service: This service is not the full service and is to be done every 6 months or 5000-6000 km. It helps to address any issues that might be caused by driving extra kilometers than average. The service includes vehicle safety inspection, engine oil and filter change, top up brake fluid and coolant.
  • Full service: Generally done every 10000 – 12000 km as it makes sure that the car is running smoothly and efficiently while reducing the risks of a break-down. This service includes inspection of the vehicle topping up the fluids, changing the oil and filters, steering alignment, suspension and reviewing spark plugs and changing them if required
  • Major service: done every 30000 - 35000 km. Major service includes all that full service offers but in much more detail. All major parts are inspected thoroughly including motor and timing belts. The service includes change of sparkplugs, cabin filter, air filter and much more.

Car/Bike Wraps

Car/Bike wrapping is a huge vinyl sheets which can be molded into any shape to cover the car directly. It can change the appearance of the car giving it a new look.

Wraps prevent the paint damage by giving an extra layer of vinyl on top of the original paint. The original paint is protected which increases the market value of the car. Also, the vinyl wraps make the car look good all year long.
