Car wrap is large sheets of vinyl which can be used to cover the car paint surface by directly covering it. It changes the color and look of the car giving it a new look and increasing its market value. Car wraps can act as an excessive sheet of protection which will protect the original paint of the car. Also, the wrapping makes the car to stand out from other cars and makes a superior road prescence.

Full wraps: These wraps can be used to wrap the whole car.
Partial wrap: These wraps are similar to full wraps but are used to wrap some parts of the cars such as roof, hood etc.
Decals: These wraps are smaller in size and done on specific area such as doors or hood to market something.

Car wraps are legal, however, different states and municipalities have different laws. Different states can have strict laws as fully car wraps can reflect light in sun which can affect the vision of other drivers on road.
